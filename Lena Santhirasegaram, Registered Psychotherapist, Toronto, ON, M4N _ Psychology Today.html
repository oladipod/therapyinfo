I am a Registered Psychotherapist (RP), a Certified Canadian Counsellor (CCC), and a Certified EEG Biofeedback practitioner (BCN, EEG) with over 10 years of clinical experience in clinical setting. I offer counselling services to children, adolescents and adults in both English and Tamil. My areas of specialty include utilizing biofeedback technique in conjunction with traditional counselling methods. I believe adding biofeedback intervention as a tool with other psychotherapy models has the potential to transform counselling or therapy, particularly for treatment of stress and anxiety.
My areas of specialty include Biofeedback, ADHD, stress, anxiety, anger management and multicultural counselling.
For more information on my practice, please feel free to call for a free phone consultation.
