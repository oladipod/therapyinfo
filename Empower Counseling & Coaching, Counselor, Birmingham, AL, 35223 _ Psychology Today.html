Covid-19 HIPPA compliant video and telehealth counseling appointments available. Affordable sliding scale rates $80-$130
***Covid_19 Telehealth appointments available. Secure private HIPPA compliant video or phone counseling. Must live in Alabama. EASY, secure, safe. Accepting new clients. If you are stuck, overwhelmed, or struggling with where your life is headed, I can help. If you are unfulfilled and unsatisfied with your relationships, I can help. Anxiety and depression are treatable. I will listen with compassion and without judgment as you share what is happening in your life.
I am advanced trained in Acceptance Commitment Therapy, with a focus on anxiety and depression. I help you get to the root of the problem- what is actually causing your anxiety, depression, or difficulty. Then we focus on determining what is most important to you and the actions you must take to live the life you want.
You do not have to suffer. I offer counseling that works. Individual appointments, in person and by video. Take the first step away from anxiety and depression and toward the life you want to have by calling or emailing to schedule your appointment.
