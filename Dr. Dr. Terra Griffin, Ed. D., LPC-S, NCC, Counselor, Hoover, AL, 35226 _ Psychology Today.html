Your physical and emotional health are our greatest concerns. As a result, our practice offers teletherapy via a secure platform, Theranest
If you are experiencing a challenging time in your life personally or professionally, need assistance working through a conflict or you are simply seeking personal growth, I am here to journey with you along the path; providing Support, Acceptance, and Guidance towards your Enlightenment (SAGE).
I utilize a person-centered and strength-based approach to help clients identify their strengths and help them utilize these strengths to succeed in life. Clients will also learn the impact that their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors have on their overall well-being.
You are not alone on your journey towards a well and healthy life. I collaborate with clients to determine what is most beneficial for their needs. Let me partner with you as you work to meet your personal goals, experience emotional wellness, and reach the life potential you deserve. Call today!
